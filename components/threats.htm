    • Understanding The Threat

    Understanding the distinct hazards that various natural catastrophes represent is crucial for the development of successful strategies. Every disaster has distinct characteristics that call for specialized strategies. For instance, storms require precautions to withstand high wind speeds and prevent structural failure, whereas earthquakes demand resources and techniques that absorb and disperse seismic energy.
